Zgoubi calculates trajectories of charged particles in beam lines, rings, cyclotrons, etc., comprising magnetic and electric fields. It includes spin, synchrotron radiation, in-flight decay, and several Monte Carlo procedures.
Sirepo development has been supported by the U.S. Department of Energy Office of Science, under multiple awards. The Zgoubi interface is supported by the Office of Nuclear Physics under Award No. DE-SC0017181.
